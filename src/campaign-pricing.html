Frequently Asked Questions
Do you offer custom pricing for specific Web3 projects? +
Yes, Every Web3 project is unique. We tailor our pricing based on your goals, product type (NFT, DeFi, DAO, etc.), and growth stage, whether you're bootstrapping or VC-backed.
What’s included in your pricing tiers? +
Each package may include strategy development, content creation, campaign management, creator coordination, community growth, and analytics. The higher the tier, the deeper the involvement and performance tracking.
Do you charge upfront or based on performance? +
We offer both, some packages include fixed strategic fees, while others are fully performance-based—you only pay when results are delivered (e.g. wallet signups, conversions, active users).
Is there a minimum spend required? +
Yes, we usually recommend a minimum monthly budget to ensure campaigns are effective, especially for performance-based options. We’ll help you find the right fit based on resources.
What if I have a very limited budget? +
We’ve worked with both early-stage and enterprise-level Web3 teams. If your budget is small but your project is promising, we’ll find a lean, impactful way to work together.
Do you offer refunds or performance guarantees? +
Refunds aren’t typical due to the strategic nature of our work. However, for performance-based models, you only pay when goals are met so you’re not paying for guesswork.
Can I upgrade or scale my plan later?+
In performance-based campaigns, creator incentives are structured within your budget. For fixed campaigns, creator costs may be separate depending on your plan.
How long does a typical campaign run? +
Most campaigns span 4–8 weeks, but we also run continuous growth cycles for scaling projects. We'll advise on timing during the initial strategy phase.
Do you offer one-time audits or strategy sessions? +
Yes, If you’re not ready for a full campaign, we offer standalone consulting, campaign audits, and Web3 strategy workshops at a flat rate.
